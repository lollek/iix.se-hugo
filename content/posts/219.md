## Ingredients
* 500 g prime rib
* 2 cloves of garlic
* 1 large carrot
* 1 medium parsnip
* 1 can crushed tomatoes
* Approx. 2 tablespoons tomato paste
* 1 cube umami stock
* A dash of vinegar
* Butter for frying
* Approx. 1 liter of water
* Approx. 1 tablespoon cumin
* Approx. 1 tablespoon paprika
* Approx. 1 tablespoon oregano
* Approx. 1 teaspoon coriander

## Steps
1. Cut the meat, carrot and parsnip into small cubes.
2. Finely chop the garlic.
3. Put on a pot of water.
3. Fry the meat in butter until nicely browned, then add the carrot, parsnip and garlic and throw in a pinch of salt.
4. Fry for a few minutes and add the cumin and coriander, stir the spices and then add the tomato paste.
5. Fry for a few more minutes and then add a dash of vinegar followed by boiling water.
6. Let it cook for a few minutes and then add crushed tomatoes, paprika, stock cube and oregano (you can also add mushroom powder and/or chili to the soup).
